I am a big believer in routine.  I like be able to switch my brain off for periods of time.  It's not that I mind thinking and making decisions, but I enjoy the fact that sometimes I don't have to for the small stuff. I was taken aback years ago by the idea of decision fatigue.  The fact that every small decision you make takes time and energy does eventually add up.  The pandemic and the work from home through this for a loop so had to re-establish things.  One of those is my morning routine.

I am a voracious news reader.  It has always a part of my adult life.  RSS is my favourite technology available today.  It has the ability to be your news feed, but I don't have to be your 'friend' to read it.  You publish and I just get it in my reader. Simple.  This isn't managed by an algorithm.  Just me.  

Can we get back to a place where that is the norm?
