WWDC never fails to show the world where computing is going.  All the updates to the OS's are dynamite.  The iPadOS update alone is amazing.  Allowing people to use the device as an iPad or a desktop machine now.  Great work by Apple to upgrade the functionality but keep the essence of the iPad intact.

The main complaint I am seeing is the system requirements for the new functionality and the dropping of everything basically before 2019.  These types of clearing out of the old happen from time to time.  It needs to happen for things to move forward. I am thinking about upgrading my gear this year.  New iPad and iPhone.  Those are my daily drivers.

The laptop is from work for me, iPad is all I need for my life.  Heck, I can do this Blog from my iPad.

There is a lot more out there, but Apple is doing the thing it does.  Iterate but make it all work together and clean.

Passcodes is going to be the sleeper hit of the conference I think.

Keep going Apple!

Keep Being Awesome Folks!
