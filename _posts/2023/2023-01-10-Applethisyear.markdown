Yesterday there was a couple of stories about the leaks for the Apple year to come.  It's going to be the year of AR/VR.  Sure there will be updates to everything, including iPhone 15, but I think it will be updates, not upgrades.

Apple needs to stabilize a little bit after the Apple Silicon move. Apple has done this in the past.  They will have big years and more simple updates years.  I think that is this year other than the headset.

Am I excited, hell yes on the headset.  I want to see what Apple's 'Bicycle of the mind' take on a headset. The big challenge is the why for this.  I'm not convinced of the Metaverse why.  So it will be more about the story vs. the hardware.  

Should be a great year.
